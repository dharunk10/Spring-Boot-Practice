
Summary
An IoT-based intelligent traffic management system to prioritize ambulances during emergencies. It uses LoRa communication, GPS tracking, and automated traffic signals to clear routes for emergency vehicles in real-time. The system dynamically adjusts traffic lights to green for ambulances while stopping other vehicles, reducing delays. Additionally, it includes audio-visual alert systems to enhance public awareness and ensure smoother emergency transit.
